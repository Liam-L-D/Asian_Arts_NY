Our Mission

To preserve and elevate Asian heritage through artistic expression. By blending tradition and modernity, we create experiences that foster cultural awareness and empower communities.

Our Vision

Art transcends boundaries, connecting people beyond culture and distance.

What We Do

We promote performing and visual arts in Upstate New York through events and exhibitions featuring Chinese and Asian artists.
